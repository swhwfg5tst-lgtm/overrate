Cargo Transportation Rules

1. General Provisions

These Rules define the terms and conditions for organizing and performing cargo transportation by road transport provided by LLC “Company Gruzovaya Planeta” and constitute an integral part of transportation agreements and customer requests.

Cargo transportation is carried out in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation, including the Rules for Road Cargo Transportation, the Charter of Road Transport, and other applicable regulations.

2. Order Placement and Documentation

To organize transportation, the Customer shall provide:

  • a transportation request indicating the origin and destination points, cargo characteristics and delivery deadlines;
  • required accompanying documents (waybills, invoices, certificates, etc.);
  • information on special transportation conditions (temperature control, fragility, securing requirements, etc.).

LLC “Company Gruzovaya Planeta” reserves the right to request additional information and documents if necessary to ensure lawful and safe cargo transportation.

3. Cargo and Packaging Requirements

  • The cargo must be properly packed and labeled to ensure its safety during transportation and handling.
  • The Customer is responsible for the suitability of packaging, labeling, and cargo securing in accordance with cargo properties and transportation conditions.
  • The Customer must inform in advance about dangerous, perishable, fragile, high-value or other cargo requiring special transportation conditions.

4. Loading and Unloading

  • Loading and unloading operations, including site preparation, equipment and personnel provision, are arranged by the Customer unless otherwise agreed by the parties.
  • The Customer shall ensure safe and unobstructed access for the vehicle to loading and unloading locations.
  • Vehicle waiting time exceeding the agreed limit may be charged additionally according to applicable rates.

5. Delivery Timeframes and Route

  • Delivery timeframes are agreed upon during order placement and depend on the route, road and weather conditions, and cargo specifics.
  • LLC “Company Gruzovaya Planeta” may change the route while maintaining agreed delivery deadlines or upon coordination with the Customer.
  • Delivery timeframes may be extended in cases of force majeure, traffic delays, or inspections beyond the Carrier’s control.

6. Liability of the Parties

  • The Carrier is liable for the safety of the cargo accepted for transportation within the limits established by law and the contract.
  • The Customer is responsible for the accuracy of provided cargo information, compliance with packaging and securing requirements, and correctness of documentation.
  • If hidden packaging defects or hazardous cargo properties are discovered, the Carrier may suspend transportation until violations are eliminated or refuse transportation.

7. Restrictions and Special Conditions

  • Cargo prohibited by applicable law or cargo with undisclosed hazardous properties is not accepted for transportation.
  • Transportation of dangerous, oversized, heavy, or perishable cargo is performed under separately agreed conditions, subject to special requirements and permits.
  • The Customer must notify the Carrier in advance of any required temperature control or special transportation regime.

8. Claims Procedure

  • Claims regarding quantity or external condition of the cargo must be made upon acceptance and documented by an inspection report involving the Carrier’s representative, if possible.
  • Claims for loss, shortage or damage must be submitted in writing within the timeframes established by law and the contract.
  • Claims must be accompanied by transportation documents, inspection reports, photographs, and other supporting materials.

9. Final Provisions

By submitting a transportation request and/or handing over cargo for transportation, the Customer confirms familiarity with these Rules and agrees to comply with their terms.

LLC “Company Gruzovaya Planeta” reserves the right to update these Rules. The updated version is published on the company’s website and applies to new requests from the date of publication.
